Rose Flavored Cookies

Cookies are sweet baked treats that are typically small, flat, and round.
They are made from a mixture of ingredients such as flour, sugar, butter or oil, and eggs.

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon rose water or rose extract (adjust to taste)
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Pink food coloring (optional)
  • Edible rose petals for decoration (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Cream Butter and Sugar: In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add Egg and Rose Flavoring: Beat in the egg and add the rose water or rose extract. Mix until well combined. If desired, add a few drops of pink food coloring for a subtle pink hue.
  4. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Mix Wet and Dry Ingredients: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Do not overmix.
  6. Shape Cookies: Scoop cookie dough using a cookie scoop or tablespoon. Roll the dough into balls and place them on the prepared baking sheets, leaving enough space between each cookie.
  7. Flatten and Decorate: Gently flatten each cookie with the back of a fork or your palm. If desired, decorate with edible rose petals on top.
  8. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden. The exact baking time may vary, so keep an eye on them.
  9. Cool: Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Serve: Once cooled, enjoy your homemade rose-flavored cookies!
